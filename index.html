About Us

Refreshing India with Trusted Beverage Brands

From cold-pressed juices to nostalgic Indian drinks and globally loved fruit beverages, our brands deliver quality, freshness, and authentic taste.

Brands Built on Authenticity & Innovation

"Raw Pressery" delivers premium cold-pressed juices made from carefully selected fruits and vegetables, ensuring maximum nutrition, freshness, and natural taste in every bottle. With a strong focus on clean labels and no added preservatives, Raw Pressery represents modern, health-conscious living.

"Paper Boat" brings back the nostalgia of traditional Indian beverages by reviving age-old recipes crafted with authentic ingredients. Each drink reflects India’s rich culinary heritage while maintaining high quality, hygiene, and consistency for today’s consumers.

"Tropicana" stands as a globally trusted fruit juice brand, known for its commitment to delivering pure, high-quality fruit juices with consistent taste and nutritional value. Backed by years of expertise, Tropicana ensures reliability and excellence in every serving.

Our Story

Crafted with Purpose, Trusted by Millions

From cold-pressed nutrition to nostalgic Indian beverages and globally trusted fruit juices, our journey is built on quality, authenticity, and refreshment.

Three Brands. One Commitment to Quality.

Raw Pressery began with a simple belief — fresh fruits deserve to be consumed in their purest form. Through cold-pressed technology, Raw Pressery delivers clean nutrition without compromising taste.

Paper Boat was created to revive India’s forgotten traditional drinks. Inspired by childhood memories, Paper Boat brings back authentic flavours using real ingredients and time-honoured recipes.

Tropicana stands for trust and consistency. Sourced from quality fruits and backed by decades of expertise, Tropicana offers dependable refreshment enjoyed by families across the world.

Sustainability

Refreshing Responsibility

At Raw Pressery, Paper Boat and Tropicana, sustainability is at the heart of every sip — from responsibly sourced ingredients to eco-conscious packaging.

Crafted with Care for Nature

Raw Pressery focuses on cold-pressed processes that preserve nutrients while reducing waste. Paper Boat celebrates traditional recipes using natural ingredients and mindful sourcing. Tropicana ensures high-quality fruit selection with sustainable farming partnerships.

Together, these brands are committed to minimizing environmental impact while delivering refreshing, authentic and responsibly produced beverages.
